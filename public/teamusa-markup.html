3D pose

How the body spins, compresses, and holds its line through the air. Cumulative rotational degrees, rotational velocity, axis angle, tuck compression—captured frame by frame.

Flight dynamics

The path through air and space. To visualize movement, the team developed new ways to render the body's trajectory—capturing amplitude, speed, and the shape of the cork.
